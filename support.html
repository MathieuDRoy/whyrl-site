Support

We're here to help

Need help with Whyrl? Reach out to us at support@whyrl.online and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report incorrect or offensive content?
Email support@whyrl.online with the headline or a screenshot of the card, and we'll review it promptly.
How do I cancel my Premium subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions, select Whyrl, and choose Cancel Subscription.
Why am I seeing ads?
Ads are shown to free-tier users to keep Whyrl free to use. Upgrading to Premium removes all ads.
How do I delete my account and data?
Email support@whyrl.online from the address associated with your account and we'll delete your data within 30 days.
Where does the news content come from?
Whyrl aggregates publicly trending topics from NewsAPI and Reddit, then generates original summaries using Claude AI (Anthropic).
